About me

A disciplined mind shaped by service, driven by data.

I began my career as a Military Officer in the Chilean Army, where I spent over five years leading HR and personnel management operations in demanding, high-accountability environments. I bring a rare combination: the discipline and people expertise of a military HR background, and the technical foundation of modern data analytics.
